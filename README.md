# Startup Funding Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **startup funding trends in India** using **Microsoft Power BI**.  
The dashboard provides interactive insights into how startup investments vary across **time, industries, cities, investors, and funding stages**, helping stakeholders understand investment patterns and ecosystem dynamics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze startup funding trends over time  
- Identify top-funded startups, industries, and cities  
- Highlight major investors and their funding patterns  
- Compare funding distribution across different investment stages  
- Provide data-driven insights for entrepreneurs, investors, and policymakers  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source:** Publicly available Indian startup funding data  
- **Key Attributes:**
  - Startup Name  
  - Industry Vertical & Sub-Vertical  
  - City Location  
  - Investors Name  
  - Investment Type (Seed, Series A, Series B, etc.)  
  - Amount in USD  
  - Date  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- **Microsoft Power BI**
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Measures & KPIs  
- **GitHub** – Version control & project hosting  

---

## 🔧 Data Preprocessing
- Promoted headers and corrected data types  
- Removed duplicate and invalid records  
- Handled missing values using standardization techniques  
- Cleaned and normalized text fields (city, industry, investor names)  
- Extracted **Year** from Date for time-based analysis  

---

## 📊 DAX Measures Created
- **Total Funding**
- **Total Startups Funded**
- **Average Funding per Startup**
- **Top City by Funding**
- **Top Industry by Funding**
- **Top Investor by Funding**

All measures are dynamic and respond to dashboard slicers.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features & Visuals
- **KPI Cards:** Total Funding, Total Startups, Avg Funding, Top City, Top Industry, Top Investor  
- **Area Chart:** Funding Trend Over Time  
- **Bar Charts:**  
  - Top 10 Funded Startups  
  - Funding by Industry  
  - Funding by Investment Type  
- **Map Visual:** City-wise Funding Distribution  
- **Table:** Detailed Funding Breakdown  
- **Slicers:** Year, City, Industry, Investment Type, Investor, Startup  

---

## 🧭 Key Insights
- Startup funding in India has grown significantly over time  
- Consumer Internet, FinTech, and Technology sectors dominate investments  
- Funding is highly concentrated in cities like Bengaluru, Mumbai, and Delhi NCR  
- A small number of major investors contribute a large share of total funding
